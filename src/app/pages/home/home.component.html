Welcome to the Todo App

Manage your tasks efficiently with our simple and powerful Todo App.

App Features

  • Create Todo: Quickly add new tasks to your list.
  • Todo List: View all your tasks in an organized list.
  • Edit Todo: Modify your tasks as needed.
  • Delete Todo: Remove tasks you no longer need.
  • User Registration: Sign up to access and manage your tasks.
  • User Login: Securely log in to your account.
  • User Profile: Access your personal profile.
  • Logout: Safely log out to keep your data secure.
Note: Please log in or register to access the app's features.
